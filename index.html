    We're One, To Be The Only One !

    Barelang Marine Robotics Team (Barelang MRT) is a Participants from Politeknik Negeri Batam. Member of team Barelang MRT (Marine Robotics Team) consist of four study programs which is Mechatronics , Robotics , Ship Planning & Construction Ship, and Non technical members. We are ready to participating in national and international contest.
